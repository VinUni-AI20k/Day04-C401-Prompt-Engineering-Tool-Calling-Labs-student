# AI Research Intelligence Focus

This demo assistant focuses on AI research intelligence: tracking AI research,
AI companies, AI agents, model releases, source quality, arXiv papers, social
signals, and safe external publishing workflows.

## In Scope

- AI research news and company updates.
- AI agent evaluation and retrieval augmented generation.
- Social posts about AI topics or posts from well-known AI researchers.
- arXiv/paper discovery and paper text extraction.
- Internal source citation, privacy, research workflow, and publishing policy.
- Digest rendering for already collected research items.

## Out Of Scope

- General math homework.
- General coding tasks unrelated to the research-agent lab.
- Personal advice, entertainment, or broad non-research requests.
- External posting without explicit confirmation.

## Demo Positioning

The UI demo should present the chatbot as an AI research intelligence console,
not a general chatbot. When a user asks for something vague, the assistant should
ask for the missing topic, account, URL, or confirmation instead of guessing.
